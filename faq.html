Frequently Asked Questions

(Aksya Coffee)

1. What are your operating hours?

Aksya Coffee is open daily from 9:00 AM to 11:00 PM. Whether you're an early bird or a night owl, you’re welcome to enjoy our coffee and cozy atmosphere anytime.

2. Do you offer non-coffee beverages?

Yes! We serve a variety of beverages including fruit teas, matcha lattes, smoothies, and seasonal drinks like our Ube Latte and Mango Cream Espresso.

3. Is Wi-Fi available in-store?

Absolutely! Aksya Coffee provides free Wi-Fi for all dine-in customers. It's perfect for students, freelancers, or anyone who wants to relax while staying connected.

4. Do you allow events or private bookings?

Yes! Our shop is available for small events, meetings, acoustic nights, or gatherings. Just contact us in advance for reservations and setup requirements.

5. Do you have outdoor seating?

We do! Our outdoor area offers a cozy and refreshing space with plants and natural light — ideal for relaxing or catching up with friends.

6. Are your products available for delivery?

Yes, you can order our drinks and pastries via delivery apps like GrabFood or message us directly for pick-up orders. Follow our page for updates!

7. Are pets allowed?

We are a pet-friendly coffee shop. Small, well-behaved pets are welcome at our outdoor seating area. Please ensure they’re on a leash and supervised.

8. Do you sell coffee beans or ground coffee?

Yes! We proudly sell locally sourced beans from Tarlac farmers. Available in whole bean or ground form — perfect for brewing at home.

9. Do you offer vegan or dairy-free options?

Definitely. We offer plant-based milk alternatives such as soy, almond, and oat milk, and we have some vegan pastries and snacks available daily.
