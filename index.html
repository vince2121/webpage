FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I rent a car?

Simply choose your preferred car, select your dates, and fill out the booking form. Our team will confirm your reservation shortly.

What documents do I need?

You need a valid driver’s license, a government-issued ID, and a credit or debit card for payment.

Is there a minimum age to rent?

Yes, the minimum age to rent a car is 21 years old. Additional fees may apply for drivers under 25.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking free of charge up to 24 hours before your pick-up time.
